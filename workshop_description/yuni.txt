This mod needs 2D Leader Framework to show leader portrait in leader selection panel and diplomacy panel. Without the framework, this leader uses fallback model (Ben Franklin) instead.

[h1]Attribute[/h1]: Scientific, Cultural

[h1]Unique Ability[/h1] - Teachings of Erudites
Have access to unique Legacy Cards which let you retain base yields, adjacency bonuses, and effects of one kind of Science Buildings in the next age. +1 Culture and +1 Production per Age in all Settlements for every City with at least 1 Science Building. Gain a Civic Boost for every Science Building constructed.

[h1]Agenda[/h1] - Romance of Arts Scholars
Likes Civilizations with both Science and Culture Buildings in their Settlements. Dislikes those with both Happiness and Gold Buildings in their Settlements.

[h1]Known Issues[/h1]
- This mod is in its early access stage and may contain errors.
- The leader framework is highly experimental since official art modding support is currently unavailable. It only support 16:9 aspect ratio with UI scaling enabled. Visual glitches are expected. Try re-loading a saved game if it doesn't work at all.
- Some buildings have no models since official art modding support is currently unavailable.
- Audio modding is unavailable for now so there is currently no leader voiceover.

本作需要2D领袖框架才能在领袖选择面板和外交面板显示领袖肖像。如果没有这个框架，领袖会变为使用回落模型（本杰明富兰克林）。

[h1]属性[/h1]：科技 文化

[h1]特色能力[/h1] - 博学者的教诲

可使用独特的传承卡，用以在下个时代保留一种科技值建筑的基础产出、相邻加成和效果。每座至少有1座科技值建筑的城市使所有定居点+1文化值、+1生产力，按时代叠加。每建成一座科技值建筑获得一个市政提升。

[h1]议程[/h1] - 文科学者的浪漫

喜欢在其定居点中同时拥有科技值和文化值建筑的文明。讨厌在其定居点中同时拥有金币和幸福值建筑的文明。
